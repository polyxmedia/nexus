# NEXUS Launch Plan

## Target Audience

Narrow and high-value. Geopolitical-aware traders, macro analysts, hedge fund researchers, defense/intel adjacent people. Don't need scale, need the right 100-500 users.

## Distribution Channels

### 1. Twitter/X (highest ROI)
The macro/geopolitical finance community lives here. Post signal analysis threads, prediction scorecards, war room screenshots. The research pages are perfect content to repurpose into threads. Tag relevant accounts. Consistency matters more than virality.

### 2. Substack / Newsletter
Weekly intelligence brief pulled from the platform. Free tier gives people a taste, paid tier or platform access is the upsell. "Here's what NEXUS flagged this week" with the convergence data.

### 3. Discord / Telegram Community
Start a free geopolitical-markets channel. Build trust, then funnel to the platform. This audience already lives in these spaces.

### 4. Reddit
r/geopolitics, r/options, r/wallstreetbets (for visibility), r/algotrading. Share the research methodology, not the product. Let people find it.

### 5. YouTube / Short-Form Video
War room walkthroughs, "how we predicted X" breakdowns. The map and UI are visually compelling enough to stand on their own.

## Pricing Model

- **Free tier**: Research pages (already public), weekly newsletter, limited signal access
- **Pro ($49-99/mo)**: Full platform, AI analyst, real-time signals, predictions
- **Institutional (custom)**: API access, custom alerts, white-label

## First Steps

1. Get 10-20 beta users manually (DMs, personal network, Twitter)
2. Build a waitlist on the register page instead of open registration
3. Start posting on Twitter 3-5x/week with platform insights
4. Ship the newsletter using existing signal/prediction data
